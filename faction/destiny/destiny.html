About the Faction

The Weavers of Destiny are disciples of Soryn, Goddess of Fate. These solemn oracles, seers, and scholars believe that all life is woven into the great Tapestry of Time. Their sacred task is to interpret these threads and ensure fate is not unraveled before its time.

Core Beliefs

  • Every life is a thread in the grand weave of the cosmos.
  • Fate is not always kind—but it is always true.
  • To understand fate is to glimpse the will of the divine.
  • Destiny cannot be escaped, only delayed or misunderstood.

Relics of Soryn

🧵 The Thread of Prophecy: Grants visions of the future—but binds the wearer ever closer to their fate.
✂️ Silver Shears of Soryn: Can sever a destiny before it completes—at a cost to the wielder's own life thread.
🪡 The Loom of Ages: Allows insight into another’s fate. Use sparingly—some threads are not meant to be touched.
