Zurathis

➤ The Shadow Weaver

Lore

Zurathis, once a brilliant and respected Archmage named Tharion, delved too deeply into the forbidden arts in his relentless quest for knowledge and power. His obsession led him to uncover ancient, malevolent forces that twisted his mind and soul. In a desperate bid to achieve ultimate power, Tharion performed a cataclysmic ritual that transformed him into Zurathis, a primordial entity of dark magic.

pon his transformation, Zurathis discovered an affinity for the most sinister and corrupting forms of magic. His presence began to infect the very fabric of the magical weave, spreading shadow and chaos wherever he tread. As his influence grew, so did his following. Those who were tempted by the promise of forbidden knowledge and power flocked to his banner, forming the Cult of the Shadow Veil.

Zurathis's followers believe that true power lies in the mastery of dark magic and that the weak should serve those who possess such power. They perform rituals in his name, seeking to gain his favor and further his insidious goals. Through their dark rites, they aim to summon and harness nightmarish entities from the shadow realms and unleash them upon the world..
