Welcome, O brave adventurers…

Gather now ‘round this forbidden tome, where secrets writ in shadow and truths long buried lie waiting.
Within these hallowed leaves is etched all that may aid—or undo—thee on thy perilous journey.
Wisdom may be gleaned, aye, and lore unearthed. But mark this well: not all knowledge was meant for mortal eyes.
For where thy gaze lingereth into the abyss… the abyss… shalt gaze back
Beware, for some truths once seen may ne’er be unseen. Some riddles seek not answers—but souls. Heed this final counsel:
What thou findest herein may be the last thing of worth thou shalt ever find.
Turn the page, if thou dare…
…but know that from this moment on, fate itself may twist against thee.
