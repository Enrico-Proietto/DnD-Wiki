Karaxis

➤ The Dread Seer

Backstory

Karaxis was once a prophet of the gods who went mad after gazing into the future too deeply. Now, he serves as the master of infernal prophecy, offering twisted visions of the future to mortals and devils alike. His realm is a place of madness, where time and reality blur.

Goal

Karaxis seeks to bring the cosmos into a state of madness where nothing is predictable, and where fate is no longer controlled by the gods. His endgame is to unleash chaos across all realms, ensuring that no future is set, and only the strongest (himself) can rise to power.
