Backstory

Azreal is the master of infernal contracts, tricking mortals and devils alike into binding agreements that always work in his favor. His deals often appear fair, or even generous, but every contract contains hidden clauses that lead to damnation. Azreal was once a mortal king who bartered his soul for eternal power, rising to become one of Hell's most cunning manipulators.

Goal

Azreal seeks to claim the greatest prize of all—complete dominion over mortal souls, forcing the gods to become irrelevant as he hoards all life under his control through deals and deceit. He dreams of making Hell the greatest empire in the cosmos, where all beings are bound to him.
