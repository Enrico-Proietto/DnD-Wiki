All vampires descend from the ancient goddess Thalyris—the Crimson Temptress.
Once a goddess of love, now a tyrant of blood and obsession, her curse gave rise to the first vampire kings and queens.
Her dominion is not just undeath—it is devotion. Through her, vampirism is not a disease… it is a vow. Each vampire is bound by blood to their creator.
And at the top of this crimson chain sits Thalyris, her whispers echoing through the veins of her children.
