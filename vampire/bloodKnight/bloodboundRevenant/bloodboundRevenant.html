Bloodbound Revenant

They were once men. Now they are hunger with a heartbeat. Sworn to the Sangrelis line by pact and poison, these half-turned warriors revel in battle’s red bloom.

Their veins burn with borrowed strength, their minds fractured by thirst. Yet they serve—loyally, brutally, and without question. They are the enforcers. The punishers. The edge of Sangrelis's wrath.

❤️ HP 85
🛡️ AC 16
📈 CR 4
🏇 Speed 30 ft.

⚔️ Attacks

  • Crimson Axe +7 to hit, 5 ft. reach
    • Hit: 1d12+4 slashing damage + 1d6 necrotic damage

🧠 Special Abilities

  • Blood Frenzy: Gains advantage on melee attacks against creatures that are not at full HP.
  • Dark Resilience (1/Day): When reduced to 0 HP, the Revenant may make a DC 10 Constitution saving throw.
    On a success, it drops to 1 HP instead.
Wearing rusted armor and masks molded to their faces, they speak only in screams and gasps. They do not dream. They only remember pain.
