Shadowflame Hound

You hear it before you see it—a low, choking growl, like fire devouring ash. Then it charges, a beast born of hellfire and night.

The Shadowflame Hound is no natural predator. It is forged—its flesh tempered in infernal flame, its soul traded away long ago. A hunting dog for vampires who fear no god.

When it barks, it is not a sound—it is a scream of smoke and sin.

❤️ HP 52
🛡️ AC 14
📈 CR 3
🏃‍♂️ Speed 40 ft.

⚔️ Attacks

  • Bite +5 to hit, 5 ft. reach
    • Hit: 2d6+2 piercing + 1d6 fire damage
  • Fiery Bark (Recharge 6):
    • Emits a 15 ft. cone of flame and shadow. All creatures in area must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw.
    • On fail: take 3d6 fire + 3d6 necrotic damage. Half on success.

🧠 Special Abilities

  • Infernal Hide:Resistance to fire and necrotic damage.
They do not bark without command. But when they do, it is the sound of war.
