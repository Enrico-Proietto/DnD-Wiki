The Seven Mythical Powers

🐉
Dragon’s Howl
Roar to strike fear into enemies within 30 ft (save vs. fear).
🌒
Eclipse Counter
Strike back if an enemy misses—stronger against larger foes.
🎟️
Chaos Coupon
Invoke randomness on pricing. Save big—or pay double.
🎲
Double or Nothing
Gamble with fate—double your goods or lose them all with a d100 roll.
💥
Critical Over
Auto crits for 1 min—but every hit against you also crits.
🌊
Abyssal Grasp
Summon spectral water tendrils to restrain a foe.
🦉
Who's That Owlbear?
Polymorph into a random creature for 1 min. Pure chaos.
